Canine Cuisine

About Us

Nestled in the heart of Marylebone, we offer a unique dining experience designed to delight both you and your furry friend. Our menu boasts a curated selection of gourmet dishes crafted from the finest ingredients, ensuring a mouthwatering culinary adventure for your four-legged companion. From savoury starters to delectable desserts, every dish is prepared with care and consideration, promising a dining experience that satisfies even the most discerning canine palate.

What makes us special

Exclusively for Dogs

Our cafe stands out as a unique destination, because it's one of the few establishments dedicated exclusively to dogs and their owners.

Gourmet Canine Cuisine

Our menu is crafted specifically for dogs, using high-quality ingredients and unique recipes to cater to their tastes and nutritional needs.

A Knowledgeable Team

Our staff are experienced in dog care and first aid, ensuring the safety and well-being of all guests.

Events and Functions

We host regular events such as dog birthday parties, adoption drives and training classes, to foster a sense of community. Private bookings also available.
